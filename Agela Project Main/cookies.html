Cookie Policy

Last updated: MAY 15 2025

At Agela AS, we value your privacy. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences when you visit our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, and other settings) to improve your experience.

2. Why We Use Cookies
  • Ensure the website functions properly
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide personalized content and services
  • Remember your cookie preferences
3. Types of Cookies We Use
  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for the website's core functionality.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics, only with consent).
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your settings like language and region.
  • Marketing Cookies: Track activity to serve relevant ads (only if accepted).
4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use tools like Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or LinkedIn Insight Tag. These providers may use their own cookies under their privacy policies.

5. How to Manage Your Cookies

When you first visit our website, you'll be asked to accept or reject cookies. You can change or withdraw consent anytime via cookie settings at the bottom of our website.

You can also manage cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari

6. Your Rights
  • Be informed about how your data is collected and used
  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
