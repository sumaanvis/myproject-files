About Agela

Empowering Ideas with Intelligence

Hey there! We're Agela—a digital design and tech studio based in Norway. We build smart, user-friendly digital products that help businesses grow, simplify their workflows, and connect better with their audience.

We're a small but mighty team of designers, developers, and problem-solvers who genuinely love what we do. Whether it's crafting a slick website, designing an MVP, building powerful automations, or creating an intuitive app, we're all about bringing ideas to life—with clarity, care, and a touch of clever.

What we're good at ?

We've got a solid mix of creative, technical, and strategic skills. Here's what we bring to the table

01

Core Tech and Tools

We work with a wide range of tools and technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, modern frameworks such as React, Next.js, Node.js, backend stacks like Python, Flask, FastAPI, and platforms like Webflow, Wix Studio, and Bubble.io. For design, we love using Figma, Adobe Suite, and Framer, and we manage code and deployment with Git, GitHub, Vercel, and Netlify.

02

AI & Automation

We build smart solutions using OpenAI/GPT integrations, design conversational chatbots, automate workflows through RPA, and create custom machine learning models. We also work with AI-powered image and video processing and natural language processing to bring next-gen capabilities to your products.

03

Product & UX Design

From design systems and component libraries to intuitive wireframes and interactive prototypes, we craft digital experiences that users love. Our process includes user research, journey mapping, usability testing, and A/B experiments, all wrapped in a collaborative approach with design workshops and co-creation sessions.

04

Growth & Marketing

We help brands grow with a mix of SEO strategies, email marketing automation, and engaging social media campaigns. On top of that, we optimize performance with CRO (conversion rate optimization) and support outreach through influencer and content marketing.

How we work ?

We're flexible, friendly, and fun to work with. Whether you're just starting out or scaling up, we'll meet you where you are.
